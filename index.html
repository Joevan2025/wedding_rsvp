What to Wear

Dress Code

We invite our guests to dress in line with our garden romance theme. Semi-formal / Cocktail attire.

❀
👗

For Women

Midi or maxi dresses, cocktail dresses, or elegant separates. Flats or heels — comfort on the garden grass is welcome.

Semi-Formal
👔

For Men

Barong Tagalog (encouraged!) or coat & tie. Tailored slacks and formal shoes. Jacket optional for reception.

Semi-Formal
🎨

Colour Guide

Dusty rose, sage, ivory, champagne, soft neutrals, and earth tones are warmly welcomed and celebrated.

Palette Colors
🚫

Please Avoid

White, cream, or ivory (reserved for the bride). Overly casual attire such as jeans, flip-flops, or athletic wear.
